About Our Lab

The High-Performance Systems and Cloud Computing (HSCC) Laboratory focuses on cutting-edge research in distributed systems, parallel computing, cloud computing, and high-performance computing architectures. Our research aims to address the challenges of modern computing systems and develop innovative solutions for scalable, efficient, and reliable computing infrastructures.
